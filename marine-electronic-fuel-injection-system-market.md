<p><p>The Marine Electronic Fuel Injection System market includes various types, primarily the Pump-Line-Nozzle System and the Common Rail System. The Pump-Line-Nozzle System delivers fuel directly to the injector under high pressure, ensuring precise fuel metering. In contrast, the Common Rail System uses a single high-pressure rail to supply fuel to multiple injectors, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Other injection systems encompass emerging technologies that improve fuel atomization and reduce operational noise, catering to evolving marine engine requirements.</p></p>
