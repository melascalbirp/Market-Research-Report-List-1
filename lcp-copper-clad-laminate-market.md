<p><p>LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) Copper Clad Laminate is categorized into single-sided and double-sided market types based on the number of conductive layers. Single-sided LCP laminates feature a copper foil on only one side, making them ideal for simpler circuits with lower complexity. In contrast, double-sided LCP laminates have copper foil on both sides, allowing for more intricate designs and increased circuit density. This dual-sided capability supports advanced applications in telecommunications and aerospace where performance and reliability are critical.</p></p>
