<p><ul><li>"Polygon Mirror 365nm"</li><li>"DMD 405nm"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) in PCB manufacturing uses lasers to directly pattern photoresist on substrates, enhancing precision and efficiency. The "Polygon Mirror 365nm" type utilizes a rotating polygon mirror and 365nm wavelength laser for high-speed imaging, suitable for fine-line applications. In contrast, the "DMD 405nm" type employs Digital Micromirror Device technology at 405nm to project images onto the resist, enabling greater flexibility and complexity in designs. Both technologies improve process speed, reduce costs, and enhance board quality.</p></p>
