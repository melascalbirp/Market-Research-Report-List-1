<p><strong>The Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is designed to monitor the air pressure in a vehicle's tyres, enhancing safety and performance. The market features two main types: Direct TPMS, which uses sensors within each tyre to provide real-time pressure readings, allowing for precise monitoring. Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, estimates tyre pressure based on wheel speed and other vehicle dynamics, relying on existing ABS sensors. Both systems aim to prevent under-inflation and improve fuel efficiency.</p></p>
