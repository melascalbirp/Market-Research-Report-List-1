<p><ul><li>Semi-Continuous Pyrolysis Plant</li><li>Fully Continuous Pyrolysis Plant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Pyrolysis Plant market is categorized into two main types: Semi-Continuous and Fully Continuous Pyrolysis Plants. Semi-Continuous Pyrolysis Plants operate in cycles, allowing for batch processing with some continuous features, thus offering flexibility and reduced downtime. In contrast, Fully Continuous Pyrolysis Plants maintain a constant feed and output process, maximizing efficiency and throughput for large-scale applications. Both types are utilized for converting organic materials into valuable products like bio-oil, gas, and char, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
