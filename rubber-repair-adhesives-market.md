<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber repair adhesives market features two primary types based on curing methods: hot bond and cold bond. Hot bond adhesives require heat for activation, providing rapid curing and strong bonds, making them ideal for high-stress applications. In contrast, cold bond adhesives cure at ambient temperatures, offering ease of use and flexibility, suitable for a wide range of repairs. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive and construction, addressing specific bonding needs and enhancing the performance of rubber products.</p></p>
