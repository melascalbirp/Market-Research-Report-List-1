<p><p>The on-site sodium hypochlorite generator market features two primary types: Brine Systems and Seawater Systems. Brine Systems utilize a controlled mix of salt and water, allowing for efficient sodium hypochlorite production in freshwater settings, often catering to municipal water treatment. In contrast, Seawater Systems capitalize on the abundance of ocean water, generating hypochlorite for applications in coastal regions, such as swimming pools and industrial processes. Both systems provide a chemical-free, sustainable solution for water disinfection and treatment.</p></p>
