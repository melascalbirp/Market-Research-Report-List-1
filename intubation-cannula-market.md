<p><strong>The Intubation Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tracheostomy</li><li>Nasopharyngeal</li><li>Oropharyngeal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intubation cannula market comprises three primary types: tracheostomy, nasopharyngeal, and oropharyngeal. Tracheostomy cannulas are used for direct access to the trachea, often in long-term ventilation cases. Nasopharyngeal cannulas are designed for airway management through the nasal passage, suitable for conscious patients requiring oxygen therapy. Oropharyngeal cannulas facilitate airway maintenance by keeping the tongue from obstructing the throat in unresponsive patients. Each type addresses specific airway management needs in clinical settings, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
