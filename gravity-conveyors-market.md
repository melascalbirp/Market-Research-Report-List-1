<p><p>The gravity conveyors market comprises three main types: gravity chute conveyors, roller conveyors, and others. Gravity chute conveyors utilize a sloped design to facilitate the smooth downward movement of items by gravity, ideal for bulk materials. Roller conveyors consist of a series of rollers that allow products to glide along their axis effortlessly, enhancing efficiency in material handling. The "others" category includes various gravity-based systems like skate wheel conveyors and modular systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
