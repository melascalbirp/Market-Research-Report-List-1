<p><p>Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) devices support individuals with speech difficulties. Communication boards and displays are low-tech solutions featuring symbols, pictures, or words that users point to or touch for expression. They enhance face-to-face communication without requiring power. In contrast, Speech Generating Devices (SGDs) are high-tech devices that generate spoken language based on text input or symbol selection, often equipped with speech synthesis technology. Both types of AAC devices empower users to communicate effectively and improve their quality of life.</p></p>
