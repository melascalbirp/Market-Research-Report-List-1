<p><strong>The Nano-positioning Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Control</li><li>Point to Point Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano-positioning systems are classified into two primary market types: Continuous Control and Point to Point Control. Continuous Control systems allow for smooth, real-time adjustments of position within a defined range, making them ideal for applications requiring precise, ongoing positioning, such as in microscopy and photonics. In contrast, Point to Point Control systems facilitate quick, discrete movements between specific points. These are commonly used in automation and robotics where exact positioning at predetermined locations is essential.</p></p>
