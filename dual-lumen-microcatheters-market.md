<p><p>The Dual Lumen Microcatheters market can be segmented into four main types: Delivery, Diagnosis, Aspiration, and Steerable. Delivery microcatheters facilitate the precise placement of therapeutic agents, such as drugs or contrast agents, within targeted areas. Diagnosis microcatheters are used for obtaining samples or performing imaging to aid in disease identification. Aspiration microcatheters assist in the removal of fluids or clots from vessels. Steerable microcatheters offer enhanced navigability, allowing for better maneuverability in complex anatomical structures during procedures.</p></p>
