<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic sensors market is primarily divided into intrinsic and extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic sensors utilize the optical fiber itself as the sensing element, measuring changes in light within the fiber due to physical phenomena like temperature or pressure. In contrast, extrinsic sensors operate by using the fiber to transmit light to an external sensing mechanism, which responds to environmental changes. Both types offer high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for various industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
