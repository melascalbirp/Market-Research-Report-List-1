<p><ul><li>Open Frame Racks</li><li>Rack Enclosures</li><li>Wall-mount Racks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data center rack market consists of three primary types: open frame racks, rack enclosures, and wall-mount racks. Open frame racks facilitate airflow and accessibility, ideal for equipment that requires frequent maintenance. Rack enclosures feature full side panels and doors for enhanced security and protection against dust, making them suitable for sensitive equipment. Wall-mount racks optimize space in smaller environments, effectively accommodating networking gear or servers without occupying floor space, providing a practical solution for compact setups.</p></p>
