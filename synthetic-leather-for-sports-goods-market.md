<p><p>Synthetic leather for sports goods is categorized into several types based on material and functionality. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is durable and cost-effective but less breathable. Normal PU (Polyurethane) offers better flexibility and comfort, enhancing performance. Microfiber PU, with its fine fibers, provides a lightweight, soft feel and superior moisture-wicking properties, making it ideal for high-performance gear. Ecological function PU focuses on sustainability, utilizing eco-friendly materials and processes, appealing to environmentally-conscious consumers in the sports market.</p></p>
