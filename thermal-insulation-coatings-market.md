<p><p>The thermal insulation coatings market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Isolated Conduction Type**: These coatings reduce heat transfer through conduction by creating a barrier that minimizes thermal conductivity.</p><p>2. **Reflection Type**: These coatings reflect radiant heat away, preventing heat absorption and maintaining cooler surfaces, often used in building exteriors.</p><p>3. **Radiation Type**: These coatings specifically address infrared radiation, utilizing materials that absorb and re-emit thermal energy, enhancing energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
