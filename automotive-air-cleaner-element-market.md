<p><p>The automotive air cleaner element market comprises two main types: dry and wet air filters. Dry type filters use a fibrous media, capturing dust and particles without needing liquid. They are popular for their low maintenance and efficient filtration. In contrast, wet type filters utilize oil or a liquid-soaked material to trap contaminants, offering superior filtration but requiring regular maintenance to ensure effectiveness. Both types serve to improve engine performance and longevity by ensuring clean air intake.</p></p>
