<p><p>The agricultural fertilizing machinery market primarily includes two main types: spreaders and sprayers. Spreaders are used to evenly distribute dry fertilizers across crop fields, ensuring uniform nutrient application. They come in various designs, including broadcast and drop spreaders. Sprayers, on the other hand, are utilized for liquid fertilizers and pesticides, allowing farmers to apply nutrients efficiently while controlling droplet size and coverage. Both types of machinery enhance productivity and crop yield by optimizing the application of essential nutrients and treatments.</p></p>
