<p><p>The graduated cylinder market is segmented into three main types: glass, plastic, and borosilicate graduated cylinders. Glass graduated cylinders are known for their accuracy and chemical resistance, making them ideal for laboratory use. Plastic graduated cylinders are lightweight and unbreakable, suitable for fieldwork and less hazardous environments. Borosilicate graduated cylinders combine the durability of glass with thermal resistance, allowing for use in high-temperature applications. Each type serves specific needs based on chemical compatibility, durability, and application environment.</p></p>
