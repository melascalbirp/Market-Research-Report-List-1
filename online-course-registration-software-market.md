<p><p>Online course registration software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely, allowing institutions to access the software via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT maintenance. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on an organization's servers, providing complete control over data management and customization, but requiring significant infrastructure and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different institutional needs and preferences in terms of accessibility, control, and resource allocation.</p></p>
