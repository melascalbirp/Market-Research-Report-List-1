<p><p>The outdoor patio umbrella market comprises three main types: wall-mounted umbrellas, center pole umbrellas, and offset pole umbrellas. Wall-mounted umbrellas are fixed to a wall, offering shade without taking up floor space, ideal for smaller areas. Center pole umbrellas feature a central support, providing balanced shade for larger outdoor spaces. Offset pole umbrellas have a side-mounted pole, allowing for flexible positioning and effective coverage, making them suitable for varied layouts and larger gatherings. Each type serves distinct needs and aesthetics.</p></p>
