<p><p>Inhalation therapy nebulizers are devices used to deliver medication directly to the lungs. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol mist, making them effective for various conditions. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ ultrasonic vibrations to create mist, offering a quieter and more efficient option with a faster medication delivery. Mesh nebulizers utilize a mesh membrane to create a fine aerosol, ensuring optimal particle size and minimizing waste, making them portable and user-friendly.</p></p>
